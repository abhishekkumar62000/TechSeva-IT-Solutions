What is an Offer Letter?

An Offer Letter from TechSeva is the formal written confirmation that you have been selected for an internship. It clearly states your role, start date, duration, stipend or compensation (if applicable), assigned mentor, and the steps required to accept the offer.

How the process works

  1. Selection: Candidates are shortlisted based on application materials and interviews.
  2. Offer preparation: HR and the assigned mentor finalize the offer details (role, duration, stipend and any conditions).
  3. Delivery: The Offer Letter is sent by email and made available on your internship portal for download and printing.
  4. Acceptance: Confirm your acceptance by replying to the offer email or using the portal‚Äôs Accept button within the stated deadline (typically 3‚Äì7 days).
  5. Onboarding: After acceptance and document submission, HR shares onboarding instructions, your first-day schedule and mentor contact details.

If you have any questions about the offer (stipend, start date, or documentation), contact our HR team at info@techsevasolutions.com or +91‚Äë94700‚Äë74183.
